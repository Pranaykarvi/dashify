About Dashify

Dashify is a web-based platform designed to help users transform raw data into dynamic, interactive dashboards. Our mission is to make data visualization accessible to everyone, from business professionals to individual users.

We are second-year students at VIT Chennai, passionate about simplifying the complexity of data analysis and empowering users with actionable insights.
